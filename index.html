About

My name is Corey, and I'm offering grinds for Leaving Cert and Junior Cert Maths and Physics. I can also offer assistance with other STEM related subjects depending on what they are, if you're unsure please do send me a message and I'll see what we can organise.

I'm a professional software developer, with an honours bachelors degree in Electronic Engineering. I got a H1 in higher level maths in the Leaving Cert, and studied Applied Maths, Physics, Chemistry, and Biology. I have also covered almost all aspects of Leaving Cert maths at a university level.
